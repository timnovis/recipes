# Basic Curry Powder

## Ingredients

- Cumin (Ground): 2 parts
- Coriander (Ground): 2 parts
- Tumeric - 1 parts
- Ginger (ground) - 0.5 parts
- Chilli Powder - 0.25 parts

Alternatively use cumin & coriander seeds, dry roasted + ground.
